What is SciText Explorer?

SciText Explorer is a Natural Language Processing (NLP) platform that learns word embeddings on publicly available text data (e.g. Google News, Twitter) as well as user-specific documents and draws connections to strategic intelligence questions of interest to Booz Allen clients. We support the DIG account under the ODNI.
